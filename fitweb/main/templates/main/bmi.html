Know About BMI

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a numerical value calculated based on an individual's height and weight. It is used as a screening tool to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight in relation to their height. To calculate BMI, the weight of an individual in kilograms is divided by the square of their height in meters.

The formula for calculating BMI is as follows: BMI = weight (kg) / (height (m))^2
